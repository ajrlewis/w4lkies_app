About


Hi there, my name is Sophia and I'm the owner of w4lkies, a dog walking business based in Chiswick, W4, London.

I absolutely love dogs and have a lot of experience working with them. I'm passionate about providing a safe and enjoyable experience for every dog in my care.

I provide services from Monday to Friday, with two time slots available: AM (8am to 12pm) and PM (12pm to 6pm). I aim to schedule your preferred time within a one-hour range of your chosen slot.
